Live Performances by Taylor Swift That Altered My Brain Chemestry

by Eugenia Garza Rodriguez

Tolerate It

Eras Tour

Starting off strong with Tolerate It live at The Eras Tour. One of the reasons I love her is because she is so theatrical with her performances, it always feels like she's telling a story I can't look away from. Fun fact: I actually got to see this live. insane.

Cardigan / August / Willow

Live From The 63rd GRAMMYs ® / 2021

These are all songs from her album Folklore which I highly recommend listening to. The two men she appears with are Jack Antonoff and Aaron Dessner, they helped produce that album. This performance just feels so magical! I wish I could live in it. I watched this performance live on tv and I cried. No regrets.

Illicit Affairs

folklore: Long Pond Studio Session

I would do ANYTHING to be able to listen to this song for the first time again. The melody and the way she sings just scratches my brain and I love it. This song is one of my favorites from Folklore. It's just one of those songs that I will forever wish I had written.

Honorable mention: Illicit Affairs (angry version)

Eras Tour

This version of Illicit Affairs gets an honarble mention because she raised the key of the song for this live version and that choice is everything. I feel like it just captures her emotions in the song so perfectly and it seems like such a cathartic moment. This is the only way to sing this bridge if you ask me. Also you can see in the background she uses the same house she used in the Grammy performance!
